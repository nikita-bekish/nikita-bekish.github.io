My name is Nikita Bekish, I first heard about web development while studying at a college where I studied POIT, but I became interested in programming professionally while studying at BSUIR.
After moving to Minsk, I decided to change something in my life. I began to develop my college skills.
Since mid-June 2018, I began to study in depth HTML and CSS on video courses, various educational applications and articles. I was also interested in Javascript and the jQuery library, then I started to learn Git. I got acquainted with the framework Bootstrap. In one of the video tutorials I learned about the Less preprocessor and began to actively use it. Of course, layout is not very fun, but somehow I know what I need to strive for and my motivation has not diminished - rather, on the contrary, it has increased. By the way, besides my hobby - at the moment, I am studying at BSUIR and at the same time I am distributing, therefore I have some knowledge of C # and MSSQL.
In programming, I am attracted to solving complex problems, because for this you need to show logic, ingenuity and have a broad outlook. Most often, I use the shortest and most rational way. But sometimes I find paradoxical solutions and this is what I like.
